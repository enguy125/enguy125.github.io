Jentle Salon Collection

Introducing ‘JENTLE SALON,’ the third project imagined by Gentle Monster and Jennie Kim. The two creative forces present a collaborative collection that transcends the realm of eyewear through its eye-catching charm designs, encouraging a diverse expression of style. Discover the Jentle Salon collection, featuring unique and unprecedented eyewear with temples designed for detachable charms as well as temples that slide into the hair.

This newest 2024 drop of the Jentle Salon collaboration continues to push the boundaries of eyewear fashion with a new collection that exudes sophistication and creativity. This latest release introduces a range of fresh designs that feature unexpected color combinations, intricate detailing, and innovative shapes that reflect both Jennie's evolving style and Gentle Monster's commitment to avant-garde aesthetics. Highlights of the collection include bold cat-eye frames with intricate metallic accents and sleek, geometric styles that offer a modern twist on classic eyewear silhouettes. The 2024 drop not only enhances the original Jentle Salon vision but also reaffirms the collaboration's influence in setting eyewear trends and captivating the fashion-forward audience.

The Jentle Salon pop-up stores, an integral part of the collaboration between Jennie Kim and Gentle Monster, have taken the retail experience to a new level. These temporary boutiques, strategically located in major fashion capitals, offer an immersive and interactive environment that mirrors the whimsical and luxurious world of the Jentle Salon collection. Each pop-up is meticulously designed to reflect the unique aesthetic of the collaboration, featuring elegant interiors, bespoke decor, and interactive displays that allow visitors to explore the eyewear pieces in an engaging way. Customers can virtually try on the latest designs, participate in exclusive events, and even catch glimpses of behind-the-scenes content from the collaboration. These pop-up stores not only create a buzz around the Jentle Salon collection but also provide a tangible connection between the brand and its audience, enhancing the overall experience of the collaboration.

The Jentle Salon experience extends beyond the products themselves, as it includes a virtual world that invites fans and customers to engage with the brand in an innovative way. This digital space, designed as a whimsical and luxurious salon, allows users to explore different rooms, each representing a facet of the collection. Interactive elements, such as virtual try-ons and exclusive content, enhance the shopping experience and create a deeper connection between Jennie, Gentle Monster, and their audience. This blend of physical products and digital interaction showcases the future of fashion retail.

Jennie Kim’s influence on the Jentle Salon collection is evident in its aesthetic and marketing approach. Known for her impeccable fashion sense and trendsetting abilities, Jennie brings a fresh and modern touch to the collaboration. Her involvement goes beyond being the face of the campaign; she actively participated in the design process, ensuring that each piece resonates with her personal style and vision. This hands-on approach has paid off, as the collection has received widespread acclaim from both fans and fashion critics alike.

The Jentle Salon collaboration between Jennie Kim and Gentle Monster introduces a delightful accessory to their eyewear collection: sunglass charms. These charming additions are designed to personalize and elevate the look of the sunglasses, adding a playful yet sophisticated touch. Each charm is intricately crafted, featuring motifs that range from elegant pearls and delicate chains to quirky symbols and vibrant beads, reflecting Jennie's eclectic style and Gentle Monster's innovative spirit. These charms can be easily attached to the frames, allowing wearers to customize their eyewear to match their outfits or moods. The sunglass charms not only enhance the aesthetic appeal of the glasses but also offer a unique way for fans to express their individuality, making the Jentle Salon collection even more versatile and personal.
